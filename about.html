Our Story

Founded on a historic Central Texas property operating since 1914, Bluebonnet Ridge transforms acres of lush landscapes into cherished celebrations. Picture ceremonies under towering cottonwoods with nesting bald eagles soaring overhead, receptions amid mature pecan orchards teeming with deer and turkey, or intimate moments in the restored homestead house and original blacksmith shop. Our commitment to integrity, respect, and excellence ensures uplifting experiences that inspire couples to linger in our shaded cedar groves or return for retreats. With budget outdoor packages launching in 2025 and a 4,000 sq ft indoor hall in 2027, we blend affordability with premium elegance for 50–300 guests.

Mission & Vision

  • Mission: At Bluebonnet Ridge, we create unforgettable wedding experiences on our historic Central Texas property, where timeless beauty and heartfelt hospitality inspire joy.
  • Vision: Become Central Texas's top destination for nature-inspired, values-driven events, scaling from budget weddings to premium gatherings while honoring our 1914 legacy.
