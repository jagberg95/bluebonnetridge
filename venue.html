Venue Spaces

Ceremony Area

Outdoor riverfront or pecan orchard pavilion for 50–200 guests, with ancient oaks, cottonwoods, and eagle views—perfect for rustic vows.

Reception Space

Shaded cedar groves or pastures for 50–200 (Phase 1), scaling to 4,000 sq ft indoor hall with river-view windows and chandeliers for 100–300 (Phase 2, 2027).

Prep Suites

Restored homestead house and original blacksmith shop as bridal/groom lounges, plus vacation rental farmhouse with cozy amenities.

Additional Amenities

Half-mile scenic riverfront, antique farm artifacts, on-site parking, nearby Austin/Waco access; rentals for tents, dance floors, portable toilets; wildlife spotting for magical photos.
